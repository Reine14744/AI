Understanding AI's Rise

  • AI mimics human intelligence: learning, recognizing patterns, making decisions
  • Now central to social media, healthcare, entertainment, and more
  • Rapid integration raises concerns about overdependence

How AI Dominates Today

  • Healthcare: AI reads X-rays, detects diseases, assists in surgeries
  • Finance: automates trading, detects fraud, manages risks
  • Education: personalizes learning, automates grading
  • Transportation: powers self-driving cars and smart traffic
  • Social Media: algorithms shape what we watch, buy, believe

Dangers of Overdependence

  • Outsourcing critical thinking, creativity, and emotional judgment
  • Loss of essential human skills in students and workers
  • AI amplifies existing biases (hiring, law enforcement)
  • Massive personal data collection with limited transparency
  • Deepfakes and misinformation threaten trust in media and democracy
